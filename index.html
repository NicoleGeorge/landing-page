WHAT IS CFF?

Cognitively Fearless Females is a community of strong, curious, and ambitious women redifining success on their own terms. We provide resources and mentorship through IRL and virtual workspaces, newsletters, podcasts, online networking, and more. We are unapologetic in our beliefs and values of supporting #WomenInTech who are chasing dreams of both big and small.
